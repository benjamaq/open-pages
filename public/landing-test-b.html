Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data private?

Yes. Everything is private by default. Only you see your data unless you choose to share it with your doctor or care team through a secure link you control.

Do I need to track every day?

No. BioStackr works even if you only track a few times a week. The more you track, the clearer patterns become—but life happens. No guilt.

Will this work on my phone?

Yes — and you can add BioStackr to your Home Screen so it feels like a real app (no download required).

  • iPhone/iPad (Safari): tap the Share icon, then Add to Home Screen. BioStackr opens full‑screen like an app.
  • Android (Chrome): tap the menu (⋮), then Add to Home screen. You’ll get an app‑style icon.

Tip: You may also see a purple banner at the top offering to install—tap it to add BioStackr instantly.
